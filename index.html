What's up with The Ronald?

An analytic tool capturing the latest misinformation trend

I'm a dashboard summarizing what folks are chatting about on The Donald, where diehard fans of the 45th president congregate. Every few hours, I pull the 500 most popular posts from the landing page on The Ronald, crunch some numbers and then show a picture of what they are discussing, so that you don't have to go there.

(To make the analysis meaningful, some commonly used words, such as determinters (i.e. "a" and "the") and auxiliary words (i.e. "will" and "can"), have been excluded from the underlining data. The word "trump" has also been removed.)
