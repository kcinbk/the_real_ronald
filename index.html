What's up with The Ronald?

An analytic tool capturing the latest misinformation trend

I'm a dashboard summarizing what folks are chatting about on The Ronald, where diehard fans of the 45th president congregate.

Every few hours, I pull the 500 most upvoted posts from the landing page on The Ronald, crunch some numbers and then show you a picture of what they are discussing, so that you don't have to go there.
